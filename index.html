About

An artist practice rooted in sensitivity, rhythm, and transformation.

Randa Ismail's practice explores the emotional traces left by place, memory, and human presence. Through layered compositions and tactile mark-making, her work balances fragility with intensity.

Her visual language moves fluidly between contemplative stillness and expressive movement, creating surfaces that feel both intimate and expansive.

Artist Statement

"I am interested in the spaces where feeling becomes form: in how color can hold memory, and how texture can suggest a life that has been touched, altered, or remembered."
